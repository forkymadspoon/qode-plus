From identifiers to shopper trust

This explorer breaks your Impact Ledger into 5 layers – from GS1 identifiers and supply chain data, all the way to AI insights and the consumer scan page.

A

Identifier Layer

GTIN, GLN, SSCC, GS1 Digital Link

All technical trust starts with globally unique GS1 identifiers.

GTIN · 00049000082055 Batch · LOT 21H23 Digital Link URI · /01/00049000082055/10/LOT21H23
  • List of GS1 identifiers: GTIN for the consumer unit, optional GLN for bottling facility, SSCC for pallets, and batch/lot.
  • Clickable identifier tags: each tag resolves to metadata (e.g. brand, net content, regulated attributes).
  • GS1 Digital Link mapping: short URL → full GS1 Digital Link URI for scanners and apps.
B

Data Layer

EPCIS, certifications, CO₂ checkpoints, packaging data

Supply chain and sustainability data anchored to each identifier.

  • EPCIS events: commissioning, shipping, receiving and aggregation events for each batch.
  • Certification documents: facility food safety certificates, water quality reports, packaging compliance PDFs.
  • CO₂ checkpoint entries: per-stage emission estimates (bottle production, transport, line efficiency).
  • Packaging material profiles: PET type, recycled content %, cap and label materials, and recyclability flags per market.
C

Ledger Layer

Immutable log, hash verification, smart rules

This is the backbone that guarantees integrity for auditors and ESG teams.

  • Immutable audit log viewer: chronological record of every write linked to a GTIN/batch.
  • Hash verification: each payload (e.g. CO₂ update, new certificate) is hashed; hashes are stored for tamper detection.
  • Smart rules triggering: thresholds (e.g. emission increase, missing declaration) trigger alerts on the dashboard.
  • Version history: side-by-side comparison of previous vs current product or impact profiles.
D

Intelligence Layer

AI insights, risk predictions, optimisation

This is where the ledger becomes useful, not just compliant.

  • AI insights: summaries like “30% lower carbon than category average” shown on the product and consumer scan views.
  • Risk predictions: early warning for supplier issues, missing declarations, or abnormal emission spikes.
  • Optimisation recommendations: suggestions on packaging, transport routing, or sourcing that could improve the impact score.
E

Consumer Layer

Shopper view, retailer view, trust badges

The visible tip of the iceberg – powered by everything above.

  • What the shopper sees: your Consumer Scan page (impact score, recycling, AI insight), driven by GS1 identifiers and verified data.
  • What the retailer produces: compliance views, ESG exports, and internal dashboards built on the same ledger.
  • Trust badges generated: “GS1 Verified Product”, “Trusted & Genuine”, and market-specific trust marks derived from the underlying data.

This section proves credibility, especially for auditors and ESG officers evaluating how your claims are generated.
